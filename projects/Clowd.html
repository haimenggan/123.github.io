Clowd

Mobile App Design & Business Plan

I led the design of Xinno Health summit from start to finish including branding guideline, website design, invitation letter design, brochure design, and banner design.

Background

We noticed people usually have problems renting items when they have special needs in a short term. As an exploratory research, we interviewed people and reviewed literature to confirm the problem. Eventually, I learn about their pain-points in general. (Methods) By interviewing different people with needs to rent items, we learned that renting items from each other in the same platform is the most feasible solution. Due to the lack of connections and trust, people have trouble providing expensive items for renting and also hard to rent expensive items from others.
