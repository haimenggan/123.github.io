Reality LA Repid App Design

UI/UX Design

I designed an mobile app user interface for Reality LA in 9 hours based on its website.

Overview

In the Rapid Design Event, I was assigned to rapidly design an mobile app user interface for Reality LA in 9 hours based on its website.

After 9 hours, I presented my research notes, key design elements, design thinking process, wireframes, and 4 highfidelity mockups.

UX Research

in the first 3 hours, I conducted the research depending on context, identity, and goals these threee directions.

03 /  Main Revenue Stream

Donation.

04 /  Channels

Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, RLA community

05 /  Key Activities & Resources

Mission Sunday, providing resouces, providing essential classes, family date night, royal family kids camp, marriage preparation, childcare, choir, discussing how we love our city.

06 /  Conclusions

The website has clear information architecture, consistent branding, and valuable content. But the website does not have a calendar for users to quickly find events and groups near them. The app design should cover the key activities and provide crucial features listed above for target users.

Wireframe

Based on the notes from research and comparative results of three similar mobile apps, I created wireframes for the Reality LA mobile app.

High-fidelity mockups

On-boarding page: The RLA logo is in the center of the on-boarding page, surrounded by planets in the universe. This design was inspired by the vision and mission of RLA, leading the users to think about life and belief. The color used on this page is also based on the color scheme.

Rounded corner design: The logo of the RLA has used rounded corner design. To keep the design consistent, the grids and images on the mobile app have rounded corner as well.

The consistency of the mobile app design upgrades the user experience and also enhances the RLA brand.
